# Math Model

This is a design sandbox, not certified gambling math.

Main loop:
- 5x5 board.
- Random symbol generation.
- Payline/cluster pays can be simple at first.
- 3+ Footprint scatters trigger feature.
- Track:
  - Base RTP
  - Feature RTP
  - Total RTP
  - Hit frequency
  - Bonus frequency

Feature loop:
- Starts with 3 respins.
- Successful reveal resets respins to 3.
- Miss decrements respins.
- Ends when respins reach 0 or valley grid is fully revealed.

Each valley has independent parameters:
- hitChance
- multiHitChance
- chainReactionChance
- collectorChance
- jackpotWeights
- baseTileValueRange
- fullRevealAward

Important analytics:
- Average feature win
- Median feature win
- 90th / 99th percentile feature win
- Full reveal rate
- Discovery jackpot frequency
- Distribution of final revealed tile count
- RTP contribution by valley
- Frequency of reaching near-full states
