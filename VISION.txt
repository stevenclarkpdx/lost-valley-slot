Lost Valley

The fantasy

The player is leading the first expedition into a hidden prehistoric valley.

The game is about discovery.

Every spin should feel like uncovering evidence that something incredible exists.

The feature is not a bonus game.

It is the destination.

Players should be excited to discover which valley they found as much as they are excited to trigger the feature.

Pillars:
Discovery over collection
Wonder over greed
Exploration over accumulation
Cinematic reveals
Strong volatility identities between valleys

Inspirations:
Jurassic Park
King Kong (Skull Island)
Lost World
Indiana Jones
Planet Earth documentaries
Commercial slot pacing
